About Me

Hi, my name is Ian (Yi Hang). I distinguish myself as a UX designer with the ability to ship code. Having graduated a BA from King's College London (Ranked 5th in UK) with a First-Class Honours, and currently pursuing a MS at Cornell University, I am well versed in designing and developing user-centerd technologies for the healthcare industry and beyond. I care about the details and consider the motion design of affordances to be crucial in creating effective, intuitive interfaces. I also like prototyping using programming languages– it helps me think through interaction models and new possibilities from the perspective of a developer.
